ABOUT THE GAME

INFERNO COSMOS is a fast-paced space arcade game where you control a fighter jet 🚀. Your mission is to destroy incoming alien enemies 👾 and survive as long as possible.


With smooth controls, shooting mechanics, and increasing difficulty, the game challenges your reflexes and precision.
