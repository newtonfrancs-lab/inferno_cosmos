PROJECT OVERVIEW

INFERNO COSMOS is a high-velocity arcade simulation designed to test the reflexes of elite pilots. As the lone survivor of the 7th Galactic Squadron, your objective is simple: Neutralize all incoming biomechanical threats and protect the sector.


The simulation features Adaptive Difficulty: as you eliminate more targets, the enemy swarm increases in velocity and density.
